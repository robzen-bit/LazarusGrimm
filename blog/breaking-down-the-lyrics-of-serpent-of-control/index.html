Breaking Down the Lyrics

Breaking Down “Serpent Of Control”: Overcoming Fear

Serpent Of Control

“Serpent Of Control” sprang from a vivid vision I had of my wife—ensnared, with a serpent’s menacing presence looming over her, manipulating her through fear. This song is my declaration of war against that spiritual oppression, a prayer for her ultimate freedom and restoration.

“I saw her bound in a serpent’s embrace, / Its cold jaws covering her face.”

The “serpent’s embrace” signifies a binding, restrictive hold, not one of comfort. The “cold jaws covering her face” speaks to a suffocating fear, a loss of identity, and an inability to express herself freely. This imagery reminds us of Genesis 3, where the serpent first introduced deception and control into human experience.

“Its coils constrict—mind fog and dread, / Mouth hovering like a crown of lead.”

The “coils constrict” illustrates the tightening grip of this oppressive spirit, leading to mental confusion and overwhelming anxiety. The “mouth hovering like a crown of lead” is a chilling detail—“crown” implies authority, but “lead” suggests heavy, burdensome, toxic power that crushes rather than empowers.

“Fear whispered lies she couldn’t stall, / A silent weight that tried to claim her all.”

The core tactic of the “Serpent of Control”—whispering lies. Not loud accusations but insidious suggestions that erode self-worth and trust. John 8:44 speaks of the devil as “a liar and the father of lies.”

“Light will pierce the coils will break, / Truth will cut off / THE HEAD OF THE SNAKE.”

The powerful turning point. “Light will pierce” symbolizes God’s truth exposing the darkness. “Truth will cut off THE HEAD OF THE SNAKE” references Genesis 3:15, the prophecy that Christ would crush the serpent’s head. This is total defeat.

“I speak release—bound chains unwind! / No shadow binds a fearless mind! / You’re cast aside—your power undone! / She walks in day; her victory’s won!”

Here, I move into a declarative stance, actively speaking against the oppression. “I speak release” signifies a prophetic declaration, invoking divine authority. My wife is no longer in darkness but “walks in day”—her victory is won through Christ.

“But love’s first breath ignites her soul, / Truth’s sharp sword makes her spirit whole.”

“Love’s first breath” speaks of the divine, life-giving love of God. “Truth’s sharp sword” references Hebrews 4:12: “For the word of God is alive and active. Sharper than any double-edged sword.”

“Her heart stands strong in sacred light, / No hidden snare can dim her sight.”

Now she is empowered. Her “heart stands strong,” rooted in divine, protective presence. She has clarity and discernment against future attacks.

“Your whispers fade before her cry— / ‘I am free!’ beneath the open sky.”

The insidious “whispers” of the serpent are overwhelmed by her powerful, vocal declaration of liberation.

“Spirit of control—be unbound! / Spirit of confusion—cease your sound! / Spirit of shame—release her life! / In Yahweh’s name, renew her light!”

A direct, authoritative command, addressing specific spiritual forces by name. All of this invoked “In Yahweh’s name,” seeking complete renewal and restoration of her inner light.

“No more veil between her eyes, / No more coils of whispered lies. / She stands in truth, her mind released, / A daughter crowned in perfect peace.”

The “veil” of spiritual blindness is removed (2 Corinthians 3:14–16). She stands as “a daughter crowned in perfect peace”—free from all oppression.

רוח אלהים

Spirit of the Living God, I speak life and freedom over her. Where there is constriction, bring release. Where there is deception, shine truth. Where the enemy seeks control, restore holy sovereignty. Break every chain, silence every lie, and restore her body, mind, and spirit to peace. In the name of Immanuel, who is with us, and Ruach HaKodesh, who breathes freedom—Amen.

“Serpent Of Control” is a profound declaration of spiritual warfare and ultimate victory. The reality of spiritual oppression is real. The power of truth and light is greater. May this song be an anthem for anyone battling unseen forces—with God, chains will unwind, and truth will always cut off the head of the snake.
