Lazarus Grimm

Lazarus Grimm is a solo independent artist creating nu-metal and conscious hip-hop that lives at the intersection of spiritual resistance and systemic truth-telling. Five albums in. Still independent. Still unfiltered.

Working with significant hearing loss, Lazarus Grimm composes entirely through visual metering and spectral analysis — seeing sound where others hear it. Wake Up is the fifth studio album. It was made to be heard.
