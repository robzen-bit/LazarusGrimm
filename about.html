Lazarus Grimm

Lazarus Grimm is a solo independent artist creating nu-metal and conscious hip-hop that lives at the intersection of heavy sound and raw truth. Working entirely outside the mainstream industry, every record is self-produced, self-released, and self-determined.

Working with significant hearing loss, Lazarus Grimm composes entirely through visual metering and spectral analysis — turning limitation into method. Wake Up is the fifth album. It releases July 4, 2026. Independence Day.
